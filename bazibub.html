HOW TO PLAY

  1. 🎯 Destroy the enemy Heart before yours falls!
  2. ⬢ Tap your glowing hero to open its actions. Each turn = 4 Action Points: Move (1/tile), Attack (2), Spell (3), Ability (2).
  3. 🟢 Your team glows green (★YOU = white); enemies glow red. Heal, shield and buff your allies!
  4. ⚔ Creeps hold the front of each square; your heroes stand behind them. Creeps & towers focus whoever's pushed closest to the Heart.
  5. 🏰 Towers zap enemies and heal your hero if you stand by them. 💀 If you fall you wait in the Spirit Gate.
  6. 👹 On round 7 you deploy a huge special creep in a lane of your choice!
  7. ☀️🌙 Day & night alternate every 4 rounds — at sunrise the whole Sandfolk army is restored 15 Health; at nightfall the Ghosts are. Lanes connect only at your own base corner, and attacks & spells never cross lanes.
  8. ⌨ Shortcuts: 1 Move · 2 Attack · 3 Spell · 4 Ability · E End Turn · ESC cancel. The 🔊 button mutes sound.
