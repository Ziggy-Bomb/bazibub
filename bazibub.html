HOW TO PLAY

  1. 🏰 Destroy the enemy Base before yours falls! A Base can only be hurt when the tower on that road is down — each side has one tower per road, so guard both.
  2. ⬢ Tap your glowing hero to open its actions. Each turn = 4 Action Points: Move (1/tile), Attack (2), Spell (3), Ability (2).
  3. ⇅ The two roads only connect at your own home corner (look for the ⇅ CROSS mark). Fight all together on one road, or split 2–1 — your choice. Attacks & spells never cross roads.
  4. ⚔ Creeps march every 3 rounds, growing bigger as the battle drags on — the top road sends 2 Earth vs 1 Mars creep, the bottom road the reverse. Push where you're strong, defend where you're outnumbered.
  5. 🏰 Towers zap enemies, and heal your hero +4 if you stand beside them. 💀 If you fall you wait in the Gatehouse and return in a few rounds.
  6. 👹 On round 7 you deploy a huge monster in a road of your choice!
  7. ☀️🌙 Day & night alternate every 4 rounds — sunrise recharges the whole Earth Bot army 15 Health; nightfall recharges the Mars Bots. Lanes connect only at your own home corner, and attacks never cross roads.
  8. 🏆 Scoring: points for every kill, tower and monster, a bonus for winning fast, minus points for what you lose. Your best score is saved — beat it!
  9. 🤝 Co-op: both pass & play and link mode offer VERSUS or CO-OP — in co-op you and a friend each lead a hero on the SAME side against the bots.
  10. 📤 Play by Link: take turns over WhatsApp — play your turn, send the link, and your friend taps it to play theirs. (Friendly games only — links are easy to tinker with!)
  11. 👥 2 Players (pass & play): choose it on the start screen — each commander leads one hero and two bot allies, passing the phone between turns.
  12. ⌨ Shortcuts: 1 Move · 2 Attack · 3 Spell · 4 Ability · E End Turn · ESC cancel. The 🔊 button mutes sound.
